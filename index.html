Diet Plan

Mediterranean Diet

A heart-healthy eating style based on traditional foods from countries near the Mediterranean Sea. Benefits: Supports heart health, reduces inflammation, and promotes longevity.

Keto Diet

A low-carb, high-fat diet that shifts the body into fat-burning mode (ketosis). Benefits: Helps with weight loss, blood sugar control, and mental clarity.

Plant-Based Diet

A diet centered around plant foods, with little or no animal products. Benefits: Improves heart health, digestion, and may reduce chronic disease risk.
